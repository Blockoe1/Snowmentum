# Snowmentum

By Allergic To Bananas
  Brandon Koederitz
  Dan Kimpton
  Tyri Ervi
  Jack Fisher
  Zaden Westbrook
  Blayne Pummel

A 2D side scrolling infinite runner about growing a snowball to destructive sizes. Build up your snowmentum, destroy everything in your path, and leave snow-body unscathed!

Everything is controlled with the mouse/trackpad/trackball.  There are no button inputs required; however, you can use the joystick and buttons on arcade, or arrow keys/wasd and spacebar/enter on PC to navigate menus.

Starting Minigame:	
  Move the cursor in all directions to pack the snowball at the beginning of the game.   Flick it to the right to throw the snowball.
Main Gameplay:	
  Move the cursor up and down to move the snowball.
  Esc - Pause
High Score Screen:	
  Move the cursor up and down to scroll through the character.
  Move the cursor left and right to select a new position.

No known bugs.
